Privacy Policy - Tick Tock

Effective Date: 26th August 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Tick Tock, a mobile application designed to help you set alarms based on time and location. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

2. Information We Collect

Location Data: Our app collects your location data when you set an alarm based on a specific location. This information is used solely for triggering the alarm when you reach the specified location.

Time Data: The app allows you to set alarms for specific times. This data is stored locally on your device.

3. How We Use Your Information

Location Data: Your location data is used exclusively to determine when to trigger a location-based alarm. This data is processed locally on your device and is not shared with any third parties.

Time Data: The time-based alarm settings are used to notify you at the specified time. This data is also stored and processed locally on your device.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

No Sharing of Location Data: We do not share your location data with any third parties. Your location information is used solely within the app to provide the alarm services you request.

Compliance with Laws: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@kodware.net.

8. Consent

By using our app, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
